You must accept these guidlines before starting the application.

(Please read carefully, there are some VERY important points).

General Information:

  • The Vancouver Cake Society ("VCS") adds an Administrative Fee of $5.00 per student per class for every three hours of class time (or part thereof). This applies to demos and hands-on classes.
  • All class prices will be converted to Canadian dollars. If you are attending from outside of Canada, you will receive payment in your currency of choice. Please keep exchange rates in mind when setting your class price.

The Vancouver Cake Society:

  • Agrees to publicize the classes, enroll students, collect registration fees, and provide a location for the classes.
  • Will attempt, to the best of its ability, to schedule a class in the preferred times and days designated by the Instructor.
  • Will provide each instructor with one hotel room at the host hotel from Thursday, October 2, 2014 with checkout on Sunday, October 5, 2014. The room shall accommodate 2 people. If you would like to share with another instructor, please let us know. An instructor may share a room with a spouse, another instructor or an assistant, but may not share a room with another conference attendee.
  • Will provide each international instructor with a letter to present to Canada Border Services Agency regarding his/her exemption from the requirement for a work permit. Instructors are responsible to ensure that they have obtained all other necessary travel documents (passport, visa, etc.).

The Instructor must:

  • Teach at least 20 hours of classes and shall teach on each day of the conference. One of the classes offered must be a new class to be debuted at Cake Love (never taught in North America before). This new class must not be taught at any other teaching engagement in North America for 4 months following Cake Love.
  • Complete and submit the Application by March 15, 2014.
  • Be responsible for his/her own transportation and necessary class supplies. Given the distance to Vancouver, special arrangements may be made for provision of the heftier supplies, at the Instructor's cost.
  • Bring a sample of the completed class project for display in the registration area.
  • Provide, along with the application, an electronic photograph, current biography and photographs of all class subjects. NOTE: A class will not be advertised without a photograph of the subject matter, NO EXCEPTIONS.
  • Accept up to the maximum number of students allowed in each class.
  • Shall carry him/herself in a professional manner at all times during a class session and shall leave the classroom in a clean and orderly condition.
  • Be responsible for the reporting of income from their classes as required by the respective taxation authorities. VCS will not hold back or make any tax payments on behalf of Instructors.
  • Attend the instructor orientation meeting on Thursday, October 2, 2014 .

Cancellations:

  • The VCS will inform you of the preliminary number of students enrolled in your class(es) no later than August 15, 2014. At that time, you may cancel any class that does not meet the minimum enrollment. Final class numbers will be provided no later than September 28, 2012 .
  • If the Instructor for any reason must cancel a scheduled class, it must be done by August 17, 2014 to avoid penalty. The penalty for cancellations after this date is the amount equal to the sum the VCS has assessed as its' Administrative Fee for the number of students that were enrolled in the cancelled class. The penalty will be deducted from any funds due to the Instructor from other classes.

Sales by Instructor:

  • Unless the appropriate fee ($40) has been paid, instructors may sell, in the classroom, only those supplies required to complete the classes that have been scheduled and may only sell thos items immediately before and during the respective class.

Assistants:

  • Instructors may bring one, or more, assistants to Cake Love. The VCS does not pay for the hotel room of any assistants, though the VCS will make the necessary room reservation arrangements.
  • Assistants must attend the orientation meeting on Thursday, October 2, 2014.

Banquet Tickets:

  • Instructors may purchase banquet tickets for themselves, their spouses and any assistants traveling to the conference for a discounted price. The cost of the banquet tickets for instructors will be determined approximately 1 month prior to the conference.
  • The VCS will attempt, as best as possible, to accommodate and dietary restrictions, though cannot guarantee food to be free of all allergens (though we will try, we promise).

Sending Teaching Supplies in Advance:

  • Instructors may send teaching supplies in advance. The supplies may be sent directly to the host hotel (Sheraton Vancouver Airport) or, for US instructors, may be sent to a shipping address in Washington State (to be supplied upon request).
  • Any supplies shipped to the US mailing address MUST be received at that location no later than 5 days prior to the start of the conference. NO EXCEPTIONS. Anything needing shipping closer to the date of the conference must be shipped directly to the hotel at the cost of the instructor.
  • An invoice MUST accompany US shipments with values and place of origin (where the item was made) included. Any shipment sent without an invoice (both included in the box and emailed to the VCS) will not be picked up. These are customs regulations and cannot be altered.

Sending Teaching Supplies back, After Cake Love:

  • Instructors must arrange to dispose of or return supplies to their home. The VCS will assist in these arrangements, where possible, but the safe return of tools and equipment is the responsibility of the instructor.
